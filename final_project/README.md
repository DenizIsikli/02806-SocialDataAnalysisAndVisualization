# NYC 311 Service Requests: Final Project
## Social Data Analysis and Visualization (DTU 02806)

---

## Project Overview

This final project analyzes **NYC 311 Service Requests** (2020-2025) to understand what urban problems matter most to New Yorkers and how equitably the city addresses them.

**Key Finding**: Heat, water, and rodent problems dominate NYC complaints (34.8% combined), while the city treats all neighborhoods equitably in terms of service resolution time.
